WHO WE ARE

Small World Games is a world-class UGC game development studio based in Bangalore, India.

We focus on delivering Award-Winning, high-quality experiences which cater to millions of players worldwide.

With a decade of experience and over 2 Billion Unique Play Sessions across our portfolio of original, owned & operated games - we specialize in crafting immersive gaming experiences on Roblox through our deep rooted and native understanding of our player base.

Our expansive experience allows us to focus on delivering high-quality gameplay that engages players across various genres (PvP, PvE, Tower Defense, Simulators, Horror, Anime, and Gacha), with 95%+ approval ratings from our players.

We welcome collaborations with brands that seek to innovate and create exceptional projects.
